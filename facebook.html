Facebook

Last summer I interned with Facebook as a Facebook University for Engineers intern. I designed and developed a social networking android mobile application, DevHub, using Parse.

Devhub is an application that brings together different developers and consolidates repositories from all the major repo hosting services ie, GitHub, BitBucket and GitLab to deliver a seamless experience accessing all open source code in one platform together with a community of developers to critic and improve the quality of the work.

Click here to view the project on github. The production SDK will be google play store once testing and quality assurance is complete.
